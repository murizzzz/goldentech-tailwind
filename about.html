About GoldenTech

We train learners to use technology confidently, creatively and practically.

Who we are

GoldenTech Computer Training College is a practical computer college training institution based in Ongata Rongai, Kenya.

Our motto is simple: Grow. Innovate. Transform.We believe technology should not be intimidating. It should be a tool for growth.

Our Approach

  • Beginner-friendly explanations
  • Practical classroom exercises
  • Real projects and assignments
  • Career and business application

Mission

To equip learners with practical digital skills for work business and innovation

Vision

To become a trusted training center for job-ready and future-ready computer skills.

Values

Talent, Excellence, Creativity and Hands-on learning
